Welcome, Recruit.

We need your help to regain control of Shadow Moses, a secret nuclear weapons base off the coast of Alaska. Terrorists have taken over and seek to use the stockpile of nuclear weapons and a new mobile nuclear launch platform called Metal Gear REX to gain the remains of the legendary soldier, Big Boss.

Enlist in the cause and change the world.

Meet the Team

This is Solid Snake. He is a former member of FOXHOUND. He is a master of infiltration and close-quarters combat. And the only one who can make this mission succeed.

This is Colonel Campbell, the former commander of FOXHOUND and leader of this mission. A self-proclaimed "old warhorse" who wants to save his niece, Meryl.

This is Otacon. He is the lead engineer of the Metal Gear Rex project. After his rescue at the hands of Solid Snake, he has become a valuable ally.

The former survival specialist of FOXHOUND, Master Miller. He has joined the mission to provide counsel to his former student, Solid Snake.

This is Mei Ling. She is the communications operator of this mission. As a student of MIT, she designed the CODEC and radar system used by Snake.

This is Naomi Hunter. A scientist involved in gene therapy, she acts as the medical advisor for this mission.
